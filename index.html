OUR DELICIOUS STORY

Headquartered in New Delhi, the company opened its first hotel here in 1980 under the dynamic leadership of Founder Chairman Mr.Suri, who spearheaded the Group’s unprecedented expansion plans. Rapid expansion and consolidation of its leadership position continues under the enterprising stewardship of Dr. Suri, who took over as Chairperson & Managing Director in 2006. All hotels within the group operated under the brand The Grand – Hotels, Palaces & Resorts. It was re-branded as ‘Restofry’ on November 19, 2008 as a tribute to the company’s Founder Chairman Mr.Suri.
